Case Studies

Remote Deposit Capture: $2M+ in Money Movement

Financial Product Launch
Challenge: As part of a broader effort to drive adoption of a Cash Account product, check acceptance emerged as a key blocker. While the product supported ACH, wires, and cards, some customers still relied heavily on paper checks—forcing them to leave the product and deposit funds at physical bank branches.
Key Decision:
  • Explored lockbox processing but ruled it out due to Stripe deprecation and integration limitations
  • Rather than building a native mobile app, we chose to implement Remote Deposit Capture via mobile web, prioritizing speed, focus, and reuse of existing product surfaces
  • Designed multi-layer fraud prevention system, aligning engineering, compliance, and finance teams
  • This decision allowed us to move quickly while still meeting risk and compliance requirements.

    • Outcome:
    • Enabled customers to deposit checks without visiting a bank branch, processing $1M+ in deposits within the first 6 months
    • Removed a major blocker to Cash Account adoption, contributing to a 16% increase in overall money movement
    • Introduced fraud and risk controls incrementally based on real usage, achieving 100% fraud prevention accuracy during the measured period
    • Delivered a high-quality customer experience, with 95% user satisfaction
    • Aligned teams around a clear, low-churn rollout plan
